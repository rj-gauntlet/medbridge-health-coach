Knee Extension Stretch

3 sets × 10 reps

Sit with back supported. Start with knee bent (foot behind). Slowly straighten your knee until the leg is extended in front of you. Hold briefly, then return.

Quad Sets

3 sets × 10 reps

Lie or sit with leg straight. Tighten the muscle on top of your thigh (quad) and push the back of your knee down. Hold 5–10 seconds, then relax. No visible movement—it’s a static squeeze.

Heel Slides

3 sets × 10 reps

Lie on your back. Keep your thigh still. Slowly slide your heel toward your butt by bending your knee. Slide back out to straight. The heel moves along the floor/surface.
